TEAM

ASHVA

Team Ashva is a Formula SAE team from Symbiosis Skills and Professional University. We are the first team from SSPU to build, drive, and compete in the Formula Type Car Race for SAE SUPRA The team was founded in 2022 by students passionate about engineering and motorsports. Participating for the first in the year 2022-23, Team Ashva was ranked 34th team among 60 teams. For the year 2023-24, we are aiming to hold a place in the Top 5 with excellent Engineers, Designers, ldealists, and a Team on our side.

Our

Logo

The Team Ashva logo features a horse, symbolising speed, strength, and precision—qualities essential in engineering. “’Ashva,’ a Sanskrit word for ‘horse’, ties our team to a rich legacy of endurance and innovation. This connection reflects our motto: "Born to Engineer, Built to Race." The horse embodies our team's relentless pursuit of engineering excellence and technological advancement.
